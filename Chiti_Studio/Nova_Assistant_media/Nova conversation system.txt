🎬 SCRIPT + BRANCHING
1. ENTRY (Video 1 — 4 sec)

2. FIRST INTERACTION

Text + buttons:

“What brings you here today?”

Options:

“I want a website”
“I’m building something new”
“Just exploring”
BRANCHING LOGIC ↓
PATH A — “I want a website”

Nova (slight nod):
“Most people say that… but what they actually need is clarity first.”

👉 Tone: Insightful, slightly challenging

Next:

“Is this for business or something personal?”

PATH B — “I’m building something new”

Nova (leans in slightly):
“That’s interesting. Early-stage ideas are my favorite.”

👉 Tone: Encouraging, curious

Next:

“Is it already live, or still forming?”

PATH C — “Just exploring”

Nova (soft smile):
“Perfect. No pressure then. Let me show you how we think.”

👉 Tone: Safe, non-salesy

Next:
→ Show 1 short visual / case snippet

🧠 MID-STATE (PERSONALIZATION)

Nova starts mirroring user intent:

If serious:

“Alright, let’s not waste your time.”

If unsure:

“We can figure it out together.”

🎯 LEAD CAPTURE MOMENT

Instead of:
“Enter your email”

Nova says:

“If this feels aligned, I can map this out for you properly.
Where should I send it?”

👉 Feels like value exchange, not form filling

🎬 CLOSURE (FINAL VIDEO)

Nova (slight smile, softer tone):
“Got it. I’ll take this forward personally.”

Pause.

“Talk soon.”

👉 Emotional tone: Reassurance + Continuity
