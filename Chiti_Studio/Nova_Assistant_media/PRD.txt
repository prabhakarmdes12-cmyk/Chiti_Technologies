## Version: V3 (Design + System Integrated)

# 1. PRODUCT VISION

Nova is not a chatbot.

Nova is a **Cinematic Front Office System** that replaces traditional contact forms with a **guided, human-feeling interaction layer**.

> “Before a user interacts with your product, they should be received.”

Nova creates:
- Presence before navigation
- Emotion before information
- Qualification before contact

---

# 2. CORE PHILOSOPHY

## 2.1 Experience Principle

Traditional Flow:
User → Form → Submission → Wait

Nova Flow:
User → Presence → Conversation → Qualification → Warm Handoff

---

## 2.2 Design Ethos

Derived from:
- Modern minimalism
- Ritualistic interaction
- Cinematic pacing

Rules:
- Slow is premium
- Silence is intentional
- Less words, more weight

---

## 2.3 Product Category

Nova defines a new category:

→ **Adaptive Cinematic Concierge System**

NOT:
- chatbot
- live chat
- AI widget

# 7. VIDEO SYSTEM (CORE DIFFERENTIATOR)

---

## 7.1 Philosophy

Videos are not content.

They are:
→ **Emotional triggers**

---

## 7.2 Types of Videos

| Type | Duration | Purpose |
|-----|--------|--------|
| Welcome | 4–5s | Entry |
| Acknowledgement | 2–3s | Response |
| Thinking | 2–3s | Transition |
| Approval | 2–3s | High-value signal |
| Closing | 4–5s | Exit |

---

## 7.3 Behavior Rules

- Never overload speech
- Max 1–2 lines per video
- Sync with UI transitions
